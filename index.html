About

VanHacks in the biggest hackathon in the Pacific NorthWest region, hosted every semester at the University of British Columbia. A hackathon is an event where a bunch of people who love programming/engineering come together to create new software/hardware! Anyone is welcome to join, from the 1337ist pro to the n00biest n00b!
